ABOUT

DJ FLUX

DJ Flux is an internationally acclaimed electronic music producer and live performer known for combining futuristic soundscapes, bass-driven beats, and immersive visual experiences.

Emerging from the underground cyber-club scene, DJ Flux has built a reputation for pushing boundaries in EDM, Synthwave, Bass, Techno, and Experimental Electronica.

With visually stunning live shows, concept-driven albums, and collaborations with some of the most innovative artists in the electronic world, DJ Flux offers a performance that’s more than music — it’s a cybernetic sensory journey.
